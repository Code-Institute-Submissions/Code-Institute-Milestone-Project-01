The COMEBACK Tour

Fifty years on from the final episode of the Monkees TV show, the band are back together to bring their music to a new generation and their old classics to the faithful fans.

With the sudden death of lead singer Davy Jones in 2012, the 3 remaining members will perform all the hits.

The tour kicks off on September 1st in Dublin, Ireland and runs though to April 29th 2019 in Fort Myers, Florida, though more dates may be added. The group expects to play some new songs, though the show will revolve around their deep catalog of classics.

"I've always felt it was important to give the audience what they want,which is the hits. Peter and I do a lovely acoustic portion of the show, like we're sitting around a campfire. Our vocal blend has become very, very interesting."

Micky Dolenz - About the COMEBACK Tour
